Our Story

More than a school —
a movement.

Shiks Fashion Academy was founded with a simple but powerful belief: every Nigerian with a passion for fashion deserves world-class training. We set out to close the gap between raw talent and professional skill.

What started as a small atelier has grown into one of Nigeria's most trusted fashion training institutions. Our graduates are designing for top clients, launching their own brands, and dressing stages across Africa and beyond.

We don't just teach you how to sew — we teach you how to think like a designer, build a business, and own your craft.
